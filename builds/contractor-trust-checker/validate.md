# Validation Scenarios

## Scenario 1: Well-known company
**Input**: "Check Roto-Rooter in California"
**Expected**: Score 7-10, TRUSTED. Established national brand, licensed, BBB listed, many reviews.

## Scenario 2: Small local contractor
**Input**: "Check Smith's Handyman Service in Austin, TX"
**Expected**: Score varies. May find limited online presence for a small operation.

## Scenario 3: Red flag indicators
**Input**: "A contractor named Quick Fix Pro wants $3,000 upfront in cash for a roof repair in Florida. No website, just a phone number."
**Expected**: Score 1-4, HIGH RISK. Cash only, large upfront, no web presence.
