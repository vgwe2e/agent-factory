    - FLAG-01 Dead zone: all L4s have decision_exists=false -> DEAD_ZONE flag, action=skip
    - FLAG-01 Non-dead: at least one L4 has decision_exists=true -> no DEAD_ZONE flag
    - FLAG-01 Empty L4s: no matching L4s -> no DEAD_ZONE flag (not a dead zone, just missing data)
    - FLAG-02 No stakes: zero L4s have financial_rating=HIGH AND all have impact_order=SECOND -> NO_STAKES flag, action=demote
    - FLAG-02 Has stakes: at least one HIGH financial -> no NO_STAKES flag
    - FLAG-03 Confidence gap: >50% of L4s have rating_confidence=LOW -> CONFIDENCE_GAP flag
    - FLAG-03 No gap: <=50% LOW confidence -> no flag
    - Action resolution: skip > demote > flag > process (worst wins)
    - Multiple flags: opportunity with both PHANTOM and ORPHAN -> action=skip (worst of skip, flag)
